AP Course Resources

Central Board AP Central

The official College Board website contains a plethora of resources including course descriptions, exam schedules, sample questions, and scoring guidelines for all AP subjects.

The Princeton Review

The Princeton Review Known is known for test materials as it offers AP review books, practice tests, and online resources to help students prepare for AP exams effectively.

Barron's AP

Publisher of AP review books and study guides covering a wide range of AP subjects. Their materials include practice tests, detailed content review, and strategies for acing the test.

AP Classroom

A platform provided by the College Board that offers additional resources such as unit guides, progress checks, and AP question banks, however is only available for students taking AP courses.

General Resources

Khan Academy

Khan Academy provides free, interactive lessons and exercises that help students learn at their own pace and has a large catalog of courses allowing customization for individuals.

Crash Course

YouTube channel that has engaging videos on various AP subjects, providing concise overviews of key concepts and topics.

Quizlet

Platform for creating and sharing flashcards. Offers user-generated study sets for various AP courses, allowing students to review key terms and concepts in a flashcard format.

Albert.io

Offers interactive practice questions, quizzes, and comprehensive review materials for AP courses. It provides personalized learning paths and progress tracking to help students prepare effectively.
